Gyudon

Ingredient

  • Two large onion.
  • 100 ml of Mirin.
  • 60 ml of soy sauce.
  • 140 ml of Water.
  • 1/8 tea-spoon of Dashi.
  • thin slice of beef.

Steps

  1. Combine all liquid with Dashi and boile it.
  2. Add the onion and cook it until soft.
  3. When the onion are soft put the meat and let it cook.
